Haloship is a 6-inch rocket designed to reach an apogee of 10,000 feet (approximately 3,048 meters). Powered by a COTS solid fuel motor, Haloship ensures reliable propulsion and efficient ascent. The commercial off-the-shelf motor provides a convenient and readily available solution for enthusiasts and hobbyists alike.

To ensure a safe descent, the Haloship employs a robust mechanical chute ejection system. This mechanism guarantees the timely deployment of a parachute, allowing the rocket to descend gracefully and avoid any potential damage upon landing. One of the key features of the Haloship is its benchmarking payload, offering a platform to evaluate their performance under realistic flight conditions. This versatility allows teams to conduct valuable tests and gather crucial data during the rocket's mission.

Haloship integrates COTS sensing and telemetry hardware, which enables real-time data acquisition and transmission. Equipped with sensors measuring crucial parameters like acceleration, temperature, pressure, and orientation, the rocket collects valuable information during its flight. The telemetry hardware ensures that this data is transmitted back to the ground, allowing for comprehensive analysis and monitoring of the Haloship's performance.
